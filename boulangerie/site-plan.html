Overview

The purpose of the website is to give potential restaurant patrons in Charleston, SC an opportunity to discover La Boulangerie. La Boulangerie is a quaint French themed breakfast café. The café serves signature pastries and breads reminiscent of Paris. The tactile feel of the restaurant transports the eater from the warm sultry south to the streets of Paris. The food is irreverently simple yet divinely exquisite.]

Home page

" CLASSIC BOULANGERIE What is a boulangerie? A boulangerie is a French bakery, as opposed to a pastry shop. Bakeries must bake their bread on-premises to hold the title of 'boulangerie' in France. (Le Cordon Bleu: https://www.cordonbleu.edu/news/what-are-patisserie-boulangerie-viennoiserie/en#:~:text=What%20is%20a%20boulangerie%3F,of%20'boulangerie'%20in%20France.) Are you looking for the unique quaint you have not found anywhere else? What can you find at Le Boulangerie? You can find classic French breads like baguettes, brioche, sourdough croissants. You can also find breads with honey and figs, hazelnuts, raisins, plentiful seeds. You can expect to find breads from different flours like rye and buckwheat. Bread is not the extent of the offerings. You will be delighted with the pastries, torts and tarts. Classical themed offerings include tarte aux framboises, caramel eclairs, sables with fillings pecan, lemon curd, gianduja and candied chestnut and pear. You can also find various citron-yuzu tartes. Come find out about the classic French food and what it has to offer. Delicate pastries and hearty breads are complemented with subtle flavors and delicate foods pleasing to the palette. Diners can expect the unexpected. You will think you are sitting in a café in Paris as your taste buds are tantalized and tempted in directions you are not aware existed. Let Le Boulangerie become your favorite place to meet friends, escape the workday or simply indulge in the luxuries of decadence in the sultry southern charm of Charleston. Your adventure awaits! "

Online Ordering

" PURCHASE ONLINE Customers can purchase online and pick up in the store. All daily menu items, tarts, torts, pastries and breads, are available online for purchase. Le Boulangerie branded merchandise, hats, coffee mugs, shirts, kitchen towels, can also be purchased and picked up in the restaurant. After looking over the menu and daily offerings, customers can choose to place an order in advance. Doing so will ensure your bread, pastries and beverages are ready when you arrive. Customers can also choose to ship non-perishables. RESERVATIONS Making reservations is simple and quick. Customers will need to know the number in their party and desired time of reservation. Simply click on the reservations button in the navigation bar at the top. Complete the simple form and choose the time desired. "

About Us

" ABOUT THE PATISSERIE Patisserie Emma grew up with a hunger for French pastries, breads and other baked goods. It was in primary school when the patisserie focused on becoming a patisserie. She worked hard at learning her craft, consistently producing fine breads and pastries. After primary school Patisserie Emma entered professional learning at Johnson and Wales University, concentrating on Baking and Pastry Arts. During her professional education, the patisserie excelled in producing traditional French baked breads and pastries. After graduating with paper in hand, Patisserie Emma, set off to France to learn from the masters. The patisserie worked for 5 years in an apprenticeship role to learn from the masters of bread and pastries. After completing the apprenticeship in France, Patisserie Emma returned to her home country with the tradition boulangerie concept for the classically southern melting pot of Charleston, SC. Patisserie Emma proved the concept in the design and success of Le Boulangerie classically styled at 161 East Bay Street. The decadent French pastries situated in the classic sultry southern Charleston, SC provides a one-of-a-kind dining experience to those brave enough to venture taste buds and senses in a dining delight. "

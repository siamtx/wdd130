Le Boulangerie

CLASSIC BOULANGERIE

What is a boulangerie? A boulangerie is a French bakery, as opposed to a pastry shop. Bakeries must bake their bread on-premises to hold the title of 'boulangerie' in France. Are you looking for the unique quaint you have not found anywhere else? What can you find at Le Boulangerie? You can find classic French breads like baguettes, brioche, sourdough croissants. You can also find breads with honey and figs, hazelnuts, raisins, plentiful seeds. You can expect to find breads from different flours like rye and buckwheat. Bread is not the extent of the offerings. You will be delighted with the pastries, torts and tarts. Classical themed offerings include tarte aux framboises, caramel eclairs, sables with fillings pecan, lemon curd, gianduja and candied chestnut and pear. You can also find various citron-yuzu tartes. Come find out about the classic French food and what it has to offer. Delicate pastries and hearty breads are complemented with subtle flavors and delicate foods pleasing to the palette.
